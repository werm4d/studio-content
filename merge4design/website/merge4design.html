Our Process

Discover. Decide. Deliver.

01

Discover

Align goals, constraints, and stakeholders fast.

  • Site and context review
  • Existing conditions check
  • Early code/ADA/life safety flags
  • Decision priorities: budget, schedule, approvals, operations
02

Decide

Present clear options and define the pathway.

  • Concept options with pros and cons
  • Permitting and entitlement strategy
  • Phased approach for occupied facilities
  • ROM cost/schedule: range-based, reality-based
03

Deliver

Execute with discipline.

  • Coordinated documentation and review readiness
  • Stakeholder and agency coordination
  • Construction support mindset
  • Fewer surprises, fewer rework loops
Senior leadership stays involved — from first conversation through delivery.
