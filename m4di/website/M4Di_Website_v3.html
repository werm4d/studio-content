M4Di.Oi™ — M4Di Operational Intelligence

A living budget
from day one
to closeout.

The M4Di.Oi™ — M4Di Operational Intelligence tool — connects your FeasOps™ ROM estimate to every ALDi™ design gate, the contractor's Schedule of Values, and running actual costs. One independent document, maintained by M4Di throughout the project lifecycle.

No more discovering a gap at GMP. The M4Di.Oi™ tool makes cost drift visible at the point it happens — not after it becomes a crisis.

M4Di.Oi™ (M4Di Operational Intelligence) is a proprietary cost intelligence methodology of M4Di — Merge 4 Development Institute, Inc. All benchmark data is drawn from the ALDDB™ (ALDi Delivery Dashboard & Backbone), M4Di's growing Mid-Atlantic community facility cost dataset. All marks are draft pending confirmation.

D
ALDi™ Gate 0
Column D — M4Di.Oi™ ROM

Locked at ALDi™ Gate 0. The number committed to your board and CDFI. Never changes after issue — all downstream variance is measured against this column.

⬛ Locked at Gate 0
E
ALDi™ Gate 2
Column E — 75% DD Estimate

First design-informed reality check. Variance over 15% vs. the ROM triggers a documented explanation and ALDi™ gate review before design work continues.

Design Development
F
ALDi™ Gate 3
Column F — 100% CD / Contractor SOV

The contracted GMP. Entered from the executed Schedule of Values. Every dollar above the ROM baseline is documented in the ALDDB™ Variance Log.

GMP Executed
G
ALDi™ Gate 4
Column G — Actual Cost

Updated monthly from pay applications and approved change orders. Running total of committed costs only — never projected. The most honest number in the project.
