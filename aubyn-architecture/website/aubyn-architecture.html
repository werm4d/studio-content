About the Firm

Architect. Landscape Designer. Entrepreneur.

Aubyn Architecture LLC is led by David C. Ainsworth AIA — a registered architect with over 20 years of experience spanning residential, commercial, and healthcare design. The firm's multi-discipline practice reflects a core belief: exceptional environments require integrated thinking across design, landscape, and strategy.

Registered in Delaware, Pennsylvania, and Connecticut, we bring the full range of architectural services — from early feasibility studies through construction administration — to every project we take on.

Our work spans sectors because great design principles are universal. Whether it's a 3,200 SF beach residence or a $70M inpatient neonatal wing, our commitment to quality and client service never wavers.

Mission & Values

Form, Function,
Fusion.

Our work finds balance and harmony between the natural and built environment — elegance in simplicity, beauty in the subtle. The best architecture doesn't compete with its surroundings; it completes them.

A dedication to efficient resource use and zero-waste design is not a constraint. It is a creative discipline that consistently produces more thoughtful, enduring results.

Public Health & Safety

Architects are obligated to protect the public interest. That obligation now encompasses sustainability of the global environment and accessibility for all persons — not just code compliance.

Design Excellence

We create functional layouts, energy-efficient construction, and visually compelling environments that increase market value while delivering genuine human benefit to occupants and community.

Client Advocacy

Architecture is largely self-regulated. We take that responsibility seriously. Every decision is made in service of your interests, in accordance with the highest professional standards.

01

Single Point of Contact

One contract, one team, one accountable partner. We eliminate the miscommunication that commonly occurs between separate architects and contractors — protecting your time, budget, and peace of mind.

02

Streamlined Communication

When architects and builders collaborate from day one, design intent travels perfectly from drawing board to job site. Fewer change orders. Fewer surprises. More of what you envisioned.

03

Cost Control

We involve the builder during design — not after. Potential cost issues are identified and resolved before construction begins, not as expensive mid-project corrections.

04

Faster Delivery

Overlapping design and construction phases compresses the schedule without cutting corners. You get to your ribbon-cutting sooner without compromising quality.

05

Design Integrity

The original design intent is faithfully maintained throughout construction. Our architects are present, engaged, and empowered to solve problems that honor your vision.

06

Innovation On-Site

When field challenges arise, our architects develop creative solutions in real time — fixes that serve the project's design goals, not just the path of least resistance.
