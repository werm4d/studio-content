## Format requirements
For each specification section produce:

**PART 1 — GENERAL**
- Summary (scope of section)
- References (applicable standards: ASTM, AWS, ACI, etc.)
- Submittals required
- Quality assurance requirements
- Delivery, storage, handling

**PART 2 — PRODUCTS**
- Manufacturers (3 acceptable or equal)
- Materials with specific requirements
- Fabrication / shop assembly notes

**PART 3 — EXECUTION**
- Examination (substrate conditions)
- Preparation
- Installation
- Field quality control
- Protection

Output as clean markdown. Each section starts with its CSI number and title.
Flag any section where project-specific information is needed to complete the spec.
